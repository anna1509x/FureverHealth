Caring for Pets Like Family

At FurEver Health, we are more than a veterinary clinic—we are a trusted partner in your pet's health journey. Our mission is to provide exceptional care tailored to the unique needs of every pet, ensuring they live long, healthy, and happy lives.

Our team of experienced veterinarians and caring staff are passionate about fostering strong relationships with pet owners. We believe in combining state-of-the-art medical practices with a compassionate touch, so your pets receive the best care possible in a comforting environment.

Whether it's a routine wellness check or a critical procedure, we’re here to support you and your furry companions every step of the way.

Frequently Asked Questions

+ How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our online booking form or by calling us directly.

+ What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM.

+ Do you handle emergencies?

Yes, we handle emergencies during business hours. For after-hours emergencies, please contact your nearest 24-hour veterinary hospital.

+ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and most pet insurance plans.
